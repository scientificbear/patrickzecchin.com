About

I am a Data Scientist with a great passion for math, data modelling, data analysis, visualisation and storytelling.

Born in Verona on 23rd September 1992, I am a Mathematics graduate at University of Milan.
Currently, I work in Arnoldo Mondadori Editore as Data Scientist, designing, developing and presenting data products. I deal with onboarding new datasets for better business understanding, using advanced analytics to deliver insights and creating models for both colleagues and final customer.
